Fast Credit Repair Strategy for 2026

Step 1: Get Your Free Credit Reports

Pull reports from all three bureaus: Experian, Equifax, TransUnion. You're entitled to one free report per year from each bureau at annualcreditreport.com.

Step 2: Dispute Every Error

Studies show 79% of credit reports contain errors. Common mistakes to look for:

  • Accounts that aren't yours
  • Incorrect payment history
  • Wrong account balances
  • Duplicate accounts
  • Closed accounts showing as open

Step 3: The Betty Credits Negotiation Method

For legitimate negative marks, use the PLT framework to negotiate:

  1. PROFIT Position: Offer something valuable (full payment, loyalty)
  2. LOVE Building: Be respectful, acknowledge their position
  3. TAX Awareness: Make it easy for them to say yes
Betty's Script: "I'm working to rebuild my credit and would like to resolve this account. I can pay the full balance today in exchange for removing this from my credit report. Can we make this work?"

Step 4: Strategic Credit Building

While disputing errors, build new positive history:

  • Become an authorized user on a family member's account
  • Get a secured credit card, use 5-10% of limit
  • Pay all bills on time (35% of your score)
  • Keep utilization under 30% (30% of your score)

Advanced Strategies for 2026

The Credit Mix Hack

Having different types of credit (cards, auto loan, mortgage) can boost your score. But apply the PLT filter: only take credit you actually need.

Timing Your Applications

Hard inquiries drop your score temporarily. Use the PLT framework: What's the PROFIT of waiting vs. applying now? What's the TAX of multiple inquiries?
